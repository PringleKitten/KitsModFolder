Toggle Note Opacity
V1 Variables:
0 SHOW ALL NOTES
100 SHOW ALL NOTES at full opac
1 Toggles OPPONENT Notes (12 hides)
11 Toggle but OPPONENT shows notes at full opacity (112 shows)
2 Toggles PLAYER Notes (22 hides)
22 Toggle but shows PLAYER notes at half opacity (222 shows)
**V2 Variables are the same**
